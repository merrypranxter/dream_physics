## Dream Guides & Archetypes

- `the wise guide in dream, ancient figure with calm eyes, knows all dream laws, otherworldly serenity`
- `the trickster dream character, shapeshifting figure, mischievous energy, multiple simultaneous faces`
- `the shadow self, dark mirror of dreamer, identical but twisted, confronting your own darkness`
- `anima or animus dream manifestation, inner feminine/masculine guide, Jungian archetype made flesh`
- `the messenger figure in dream, carrying cryptic knowledge, symbolic object in hand, threshold guardian`

## Composite & Morphing Figures

- `dream composite character, features of multiple people merged impossibly, uncanny hybrid face`
- `shape-shifting dream figure, fluid identity, face changing mid-conversation`
- `dream double, exact copy of self encountered in dream, meeting your own doppelganger`
- `the crowd that is one person, many faces all belonging to the same entity, collective being`

## Dream Creatures & Entities

- `sleep paralysis entity, dark formless presence in peripheral vision, ancient terror, shadow being`
- `hypnagogic hallucination figure, half-formed being from threshold consciousness, phosphene entity`
- `the pursuer in a dream, undefined threat chasing through impossible landscape, primal fear`
- `dream animal guide, spirit animal with luminous eyes, symbolic creature companion`
- `the watcher in dream, figure who observes but never acts, cosmic observer, detached witness`

## Non-Human Dream Characters

- `dream city as living being, architecture that breathes and shifts, intelligent environment`
- `symbolic object that has become a character, the door that knows things, the clock that speaks`
- `collective dream entity, all characters merging into single organism, hive mind consciousness`
